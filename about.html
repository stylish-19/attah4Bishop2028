About

Rev. Dr. Alvin E. Attah

A devoted servant of God, seasoned educator, and transformational leader within the African Methodist Episcopal (AME) Church. My mission is to serve God’s people, foster community growth, and raise leaders who will shape a brighter tomorrow.

With decades of ministry experience across Africa and beyond, my journey has been defined by faithful service, unwavering integrity, and a passionate commitment to the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Through preaching, teaching, and leadership, I have witnessed lives transformed, churches strengthened, and communities empowered for lasting impact.

As I look toward 2028, when I prayerfully offer myself as a candidate for Bishop, my heart remains steadfast—to be a servant leader for a great church. My vision is to guide the AME Church into a future where faith, innovation, and compassion work together to uplift people, nurture spiritual growth, and equip the next generation of leaders.
